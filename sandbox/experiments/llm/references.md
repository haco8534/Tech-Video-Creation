# 参考文献

- Attention Is All You Need — Ashish Vaswani et al., 2017, https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762: Transformer と Attention を説明する根拠
- Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners — Alec Radford et al., OpenAI, 2019, https://cdn.openai.com/better-language-models/language_models_are_unsupervised_multitask_learners.pdf: 言語モデルが次トークン予測から多様なタスク能力を得る説明の根拠
- Language Models are Few-Shot Learners — Tom B. Brown et al., OpenAI, 2020, https://arxiv.org/abs/2005.14165: few-shot / in-context learning と、重み更新なしで例に合わせる説明の根拠
- Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning in Large Language Models — Jason Wei et al., 2022, https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903: 中間ステップを文脈に置くと複雑な推論がしやすくなる説明の根拠
- On the Dangers of Stochastic Parrots: Can Language Models Be Too Big? — Emily M. Bender et al., 2021, https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3442188.3445922: 言語モデルの限界、意味理解への注意、自然に見える誤りのリスクを扱う補助資料
